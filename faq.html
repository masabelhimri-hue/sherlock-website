During the inspection
Most inspections take between 2 and 4 hours depending on the size and age of the home. Larger or older homes may take longer. We never rush — thoroughness is the point.
We strongly encourage it. Being present allows you to see findings firsthand, ask questions in real time, and get a much deeper understanding of the home than any written report can provide on its own.
We inspect all major systems and components including the roof, attic, foundation, crawl space, structural framing, electrical system, plumbing, HVAC, windows, doors, and more. See our Services page for a full breakdown.
Standard inspections do not include specialized tests such as radon, mold, asbestos, sewer scopes, or pest/termite inspections. These are separate services. We're happy to recommend trusted specialists for any of these.
After the inspection
Your full, detailed report is delivered within 24 hours of the inspection — typically the same evening. It includes written findings, photos, and clear descriptions of any issues found.
Reports are delivered digitally and are easy to read — written for homebuyers, not contractors. They include categorized findings, photos of every issue noted, and clear explanations of what each finding means and whether it needs immediate attention.
