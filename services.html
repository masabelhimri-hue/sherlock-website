What we inspect
Every system. Every detail.

A Sherlock inspection covers the full home — from the roof to the crawl space — so you have a complete picture before you commit.

Proudly serving King · Pierce · Snohomish · Skagit · Thurston · Lewis · Kitsap Counties

🏗️

Structural & Foundation

Structural
Foundation & Structure

The bones of the home. We assess framing, foundation, and structural integrity — identifying settlement, cracks, moisture intrusion, and anything that could affect the long-term stability of the property.

  • ✓ Foundation walls, footings, and settlement
  • ✓ Crawl space condition and moisture
  • ✓ Floor, wall, and roof framing
  • ✓ Visible signs of water intrusion or damage
🏠

Roof & Attic

Exterior
Roof & Attic

In the Pacific Northwest, your roof works hard. We inspect shingles, flashing, gutters, and attic ventilation — giving you a clear picture of remaining life and any issues that need attention now or soon.

  • ✓ Shingles, flashing, and ridge condition
  • ✓ Gutters, downspouts, and drainage
  • ✓ Attic insulation and ventilation
  • ✓ Signs of water damage or leaks
⚡

Electrical Systems

Electrical
Electrical Systems

Electrical issues are among the most common — and potentially dangerous — findings in a home. We inspect the panel, wiring, outlets, and safety devices to identify hazards, outdated components, and code concerns.

  • ✓ Main panel and circuit breakers
  • ✓ Wiring type and condition
  • ✓ GFCI and AFCI protection
  • ✓ Outlets, switches, and fixtures
💧

Plumbing

Plumbing
Plumbing Systems

From the water main to the fixtures, we check for leaks, water pressure, drainage performance, and aging materials. We also inspect the water heater, including its age, condition, and safety features.

  • ✓ Supply lines, shutoff valves, and pressure
  • ✓ Drains, waste, and vent systems
  • ✓ Water heater age, condition, and safety
  • ✓ Fixtures, faucets, and visible supply lines
🌡️

HVAC Systems

HVAC
Heating, Cooling & Ventilation

We test and evaluate your heating and cooling systems for function, safety, and remaining service life — helping you understand what's working, what needs maintenance, and what may need replacement soon.

  • ✓ Furnace / heat pump operation and age
  • ✓ Air conditioning (seasonal)
  • ✓ Ductwork and air distribution
  • ✓ Exhaust fans and ventilation
