What we offer
Every Service You Need,
Under One Roof

From full home inspections to specialized environmental testing — we handle it all so you can buy with confidence.

🏠
Full Home Inspection

A complete examination of every major system — roof to foundation, attic to crawl space. Our most thorough offering.

🔩
Major Components Inspection

Focused assessment of the home's key systems — structure, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC — for a targeted, efficient review.

🚽
Sewer Scope

Camera inspection of the main sewer line to identify blockages, root intrusion, cracks, and other costly hidden issues.

💧
Water Sampling

Lab-tested water samples to detect contaminants, bacteria, heavy metals, and other quality concerns in your water supply.

🌦️
Air Quality (Mold)

Air and surface sampling to detect mold spores and assess indoor air quality — especially important in the Pacific Northwest climate.

🔬
Asbestos Testing

Material sampling and lab analysis to identify asbestos in older homes before renovation or purchase — critical for pre-1980s properties.

🎨
Lead Testing

Paint and surface testing for lead hazards, particularly in homes built before 1978. Safe, fast, and lab-verified results.

🛡️
Warranty Inspection

11-month builder warranty inspection to document defects before your coverage expires — catch issues while someone else still pays.
